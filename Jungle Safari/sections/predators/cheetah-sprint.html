The Terminal Velocity: Cheetah

The cheetah is evolution's specialized speedster. Built for short, high-speed bursts, it can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just three seconds, faster than most sports cars.

Aerodynamic Engineering

Non-retractable claws act like cleats, while the long tail serves as a rudder for steering during high-speed chases.

The Cost of Speed

The cheetah's light frame makes it vulnerable to larger predators. It must eat quickly before scavengers arrive.

Daylight Hunters

To avoid competition with lions and hyenas, cheetahs hunt during the day. They rely on their keen eyesight to spot prey from a distance and their incredible agility to finish the chase.
