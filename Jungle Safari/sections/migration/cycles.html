The Cycle of Life

The Great Migration is a 12-month journey. Between January and March, the herds are in the southern Serengeti for calving season. Within a few weeks, almost 500,000 wildebeest calves are born, attracting predators from all over.

Calving Season

A time of abundance and extreme vulnerability. Newborns must be able to run within minutes of birth.

The Rut

Occurring around May, this is the breeding season where males compete for dominance and territory.

The Northern Trek

By June and July, the rain-parched plains force the herds north. They cross the Grumeti and Mara rivers, eventually reaching the green pastures of the Maasai Mara in Kenya by August, where they stay until the short rains return in October.
