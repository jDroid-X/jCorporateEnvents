SkyAuto

SkyAuto is the transportation layer of the AI World. We believe that movement should be a seamless, silent infrastructure that serves humanity without friction.

Our Working Style

We practice "Kinetic Excellence." At SkyAuto, we don't just write code; we live in motion. Our development cycle is based on real-world simulations within jDroid-X’s massive digital-twin cities. We prioritize safety above all else, employing a 'triple-redundant' logic system where three independent AIs must agree on every critical movement. Our teams are highly mobile, often working from within the very autonomous vehicles they are refining.

Product 1: AeroPilot

"Own the Sky."

AeroPilot is the world's first fully autonomous flight controller for urban air mobility. It manages everything from VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) logistics to bird-strike avoidance. It ensures that the sky remains an orderly, safe, and silent corridor for the transport of both people and essential goods.

Product 2: SwiftGlide

"The Silent Commute."

SwiftGlide is a family of terrestrial autonomous platforms designed for the 'last mile.' These sleek, low-profile pods use magnetic levitation and AI-based swarm logic to navigate dense urban centers without ever stopping at a red light, optimizing the city's heartbeat.

Product 3: OmniTraffic

"The Orchestra of Motion."

OmniTraffic is an enterprise city-management AI. It connects all vehicles, pedestrians, and infrastructure into a single living network. By predicting traffic surges before they happen, OmniTraffic dynamically adjusts speed limits and route suggestions to ensure 100% flow efficiency.
