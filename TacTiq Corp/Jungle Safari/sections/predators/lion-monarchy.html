Social Monarchy: The Pride

Lions are the only truly social cats. A pride is a complex family unit consisting of related females, their offspring, and a small group of resident males who defend the territory.

The Matriarchy

Females are the heart of the pride. They hunt together, raise cubs communally, and remain with the pride for life.

The Defenders

Males primary role is protection. They patrol borders, ward off nomadic rivals, and ensure the safety of the bloodline.

The Cooperative Hunter

When hunting large prey like buffalo or giraffes, the lionesses work in unison. They use flanking maneuvers and strategic positioning to corner their target. Despite their size, they are masters of the "stalk and pounce" technique.
