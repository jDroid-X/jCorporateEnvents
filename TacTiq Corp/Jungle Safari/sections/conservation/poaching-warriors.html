Guardian Warriors: Anti-Poaching

In the fight for survival, the anti-poaching units (APUs) are the thin green line. These dedicated men and women spend weeks at a time patrolling the vast wilderness to protect endangered species from illegal hunting.

Protection Units

Armed with modern technology and deep tracking knowledge, they monitor rhino and elephant populations 24/7.

K9 Support

Highly trained tracking dogs are essential partners, able to pick up scents that humans would miss.

A Dangerous Calling

The work is dangerous, as poachers are often armed and desperate. Yet, the commitment of these warriors has led to significant recoveries in rhino populations in several key areas.
